Deli / Kitchen Member

location_on Foodie's Corner - Liberty Hill schedule Full-Time / Part-Time

Position Summary

Deli Workers at Foodies Corner are passionate about great food and excellent service. This role is responsible for preparing and serving fresh, high-quality deli items while maintaining a clean and organized kitchen workspace and ensuring every customer leaves satisfied.

Key Responsibilities

  • check_circle Prepare deli items including sandwiches, hot foods, salads, and other menu offerings to order and in advance
  • check_circle Follow all recipes, portion guidelines, and presentation standards consistently
  • check_circle Operate deli equipment including slicers, grills, fryers, ovens, and warmers safely and correctly
  • check_circle Maintain proper food temperatures and complete required temperature logs throughout the shift
  • check_circle Label, date, and properly store all food items following FIFO (First In, First Out) rotation
  • check_circle Keep the deli counter, prep area, and kitchen clean and organized at all times
  • check_circle Assist customers at the deli counter, take orders, and answer questions about menu items
  • check_circle Restock deli displays, grab-and-go cases, and hot food sections as needed
  • check_circle Notify the Deli/Kitchen Manager of low inventory, equipment issues, or food quality concerns
  • check_circle Follow all food safety, sanitation, and personal hygiene standards at all times
  • check_circle Assist with receiving and storing food deliveries as directed
  • check_circle Complete assigned opening, mid-shift, and closing cleaning and prep duties
  • check_circle Support other store departments during slow periods or as requested by management

Requirements

  • arrow_right Prior food service or deli experience preferred but not required — we will train!
  • arrow_right Food handler certification (or willingness to obtain upon hire)
  • arrow_right Ability to stand for long periods and work in a fast-paced environment
  • arrow_right Team-oriented with strong attention to detail
  • arrow_right Ability to work flexible hours including early mornings, weekends, and holidays
